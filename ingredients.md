Ingredients:

	•	For the syrup:
	•	1 1/2 cups sugar
	•	1 1/2 cups water
	•	1/2 cup honey
	•	1 teaspoon vanilla extract
	•	1 teaspoon lemon juice
	•	For the baklava:
	•	1 package (16 oz) phyllo dough, thawed
	•	2 cups nuts (walnuts, pistachios, or a mix), finely chopped
	•	1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
	•	1 cup unsalted butter, melted
